Chapter 0, Ceres:




Like every other planet in the universe, Ceres, name given to this planet that for some odd reason always ends up being the same name that every dweller generation chooses: was created from some kind of meteor collision or whatever.

But unlike other habitable planets, this one is not, its core is way too unstable and there is just no way for life to emerge on this planet.
