Snoop the weird girl's

You start snooping around the various tabs the girl left opened in her browser.

APFEL: Looks like she was reading some kind of vampire literature...

All the books you used to read back in your being-awake-world were about science or alchemy. Very few times did you find a fantasy book lying around in the forest, and none of those you found were about vampires. You guess that you should search more deeply next time; these guys look interesting.
